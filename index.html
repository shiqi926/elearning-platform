About Us

Hi there, Welcome to Finals Fantasy!

Finals Fantasy aims to value-add to the education of primary school children by giving them additional sources of learning in light of the Covid-19 situation. By incorporating a retro 8-bit style, we hope to entice young children to use our platform and make learning more exciting. Students will be allowed to "walk" around a virtual school and attend classes through interactive quizzes, just like how they do usually.

How it works?

How this works?

START

Click on "PRESS START" to login, or sign up for an account if you don't already have one.

DASHBOARD

After logging in, your personal dashboard is displayed, where you can track your math and science progress!

LEARN

Click on "Learn" to review topics taught in school or to pick up new concepts.

TEST

When you’re ready, click on "Game" and walk to math or science school to take a quiz! Choose between easy, medium and hard to test your knowledge.
